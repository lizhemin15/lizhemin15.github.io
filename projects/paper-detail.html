RSS: Toolbox of Differentiable Signal and Regularization Representation

Zhemin Li
Project start at 2023

Abstract

This paper presents a differentiable signal and regularization representation toolbox (RSS) for handling signal representation problems in high-dimensional data. The toolbox combines the advantages of deep learning and traditional signal processing methods, providing a complete differentiable algorithmic framework. By introducing differentiable regularization terms, our method can achieve end-to-end optimization while maintaining signal structure. Experimental results show that RSS achieves superior performance compared to existing methods on multiple benchmark datasets, particularly excelling in processing high-dimensional sparse signals.
